# Deep Reader Output Format

## notes.md full example

```markdown
# Deep Reader — Notes

## Papers Read
### Author2024 — Title (DOI: 10.xxxx) [pages 8-18 of 24 — PARTIAL, resume at page 19]
- Key finding 1
- **Quantitative data:** [MANDATORY — specific numbers, not vague descriptions]
- Contradicts: [other paper] on [topic]
- Reading plan: skipped pages 1-7 (intro/background), started at Experimental Setup

## Unread Queue
- Author2023 — Title (A-grade, papers/Author2023_Title.pdf)
  Plan: pages 5-12 (Results, high priority)

## Discovered References (not yet in corpus)
- "Title..." — cited by X, relevant because [reason]
  STATUS: queued for gap fill

## Emerging Synthesis
- [Cross-paper patterns, updated each iteration]

## Open Problems Identified
- [Accumulated gap observations with evidence]

## Figure Opportunities
- [Dataset/comparison that would make a compelling figure]
  Data: [specific numbers, table references]
  Type suggestion: [timeline / bar chart / scatter / comparison table]
  Source papers: [Author2024, Author2023]

## Extracted Reference Figures
- `reference-figures/Author2024_p8_img1.png` — [what this figure shows, why it was extracted]
```
